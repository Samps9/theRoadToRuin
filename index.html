1/18/17

New Album -- Hearbeat From the Next Step

Song a Day Release: Not All Time Spent Idly Is Wasted

Today's song is called "Not All Time Spent Idly Is Wasted". Miles recorded all the layered keyboard lines on a Yamaha CP-35 he had recently acquired. We were actually just testing it out but we liked what we came up with so it grew into a full recording. The song itself Miles had written about a month before and had planned to make it an acoustic sort of thing, the final recording turned out to be very different from the initial vision. The lyrics are very personal to Miles and discuss a line of thinking he often finds himself following.

Here is "Useless", the second song on Heartbeat From The Next Step! Sung by Katie Ortiz, this recording was very collaborative. Sam Lubin's bass line set up the rhythm track and we all came up with an instrumental part to highlight it. The result was all grooves and negative space, enjoy!!!
